Philosophy

How I work

Engineering principles that guide how I design systems and lead teams.

01
Production over Proof-of-Concept
AI systems must work in the real world — with real data, real edge cases, and real users. I design for production from day one: observability, guardrails, fallbacks, and measurable accuracy targets.
02
Security & Guardrails First
In healthcare and enterprise environments, security isn't optional. Every AI system I build includes guardrails, data governance, and compliance-aware design — not as an afterthought.
03
Cross-Timezone Leadership
Having worked daily with US teams for 3+ years, I know that distributed engineering works best with clear documentation, async-first communication, and shared architectural context.
04
Mentorship & Technical Growth
The best technical leverage is in raising the people around you. I invest in mentoring engineers to think in systems, write maintainable code, and own their craft end-to-end.
